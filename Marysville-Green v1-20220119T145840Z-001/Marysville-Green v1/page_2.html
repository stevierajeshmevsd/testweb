Traveling in Marysville

Traveling is a big part of the emission of greenhouse gases, but in a small town like Marysville, it is a necessity to own a car to get around, otherwise it's very hard to get to places as Marysville does not have any public transportation. But there are ways to combat this as a community. Carpooling is a good way to save gas money and help save our earth. You can also walk or bike to your destination if doable.

Suggestions

A good way to travel downtown would be to ride a bike using the John Simmons trail, which would connect to schwartzkopf park. You could then ride a short distance and you would reach downtown.
